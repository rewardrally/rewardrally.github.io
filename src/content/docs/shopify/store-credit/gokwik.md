The **GoKwik Store Credit Integration** allows you to seamlessly sync RewardRally points with GoKwik’s checkout and refund ecosystem.

## What it is

This integration ensures that reward points earned in RewardRally can be used as store credit during a GoKwik checkout. It helps reduce checkout abandonment by making rewards instantly accessible.

### Why use it?

- **Faster Checkout:** GoKwik is known for its "one-click" checkout. Adding RewardRally credits makes it even more appealing for returning customers.
- **Synchronized Balances:** Spending credit via GoKwik automatically updates the RewardRally wallet.
- **Unified Experience:** Customers see a single, consistent store credit balance whether they are on your site or in the GoKwik checkout window.

### Customer Experience

1.  **Earning:** A customer earns points through orders, welcome bonuses, or referrals.
2.  **Conversion:** RewardRally converts those points into your store's currency (e.g., INR).
3.  **Spending:** During the GoKwik checkout process, the customer's available store credit is automatically applied or shown as an option.

---

## How to enable it

Follow these steps to link RewardRally with your GoKwik account.

### Step 1: Configure Your Conversion Rates

RewardRally uses two specific rates to manage your program's economy:

1.  **Point Conversion (Earning):** This determines how many points a customer earns based on their spend (e.g., **₹100 = 5 points**).  
    *PS: This is set in your **Shopify RewardRally Admin Dashboard.***

2.  **Coin Conversion Rate (Redeeming):** This determines the monetary value of those points when they are turned into store credit (e.g., **5 points = 5 coins/₹5.00**).  
    *PS: This is set in your **RewardRally Admin Dashboard.***

### Step 2: Contact RewardRally for Initial Setup

Unlike other integrations, you do not need to change settings in your Shopify Admin for GoKwik. Instead, the connection is established within your GoKwik Merchant portal.

Since RewardRally is currently in a specialized rollout phase for GoKwik, please contact our support team to handle the initial technical configuration for your store.

1.  Log in to your **GoKwik Merchant Dashboard** to ensure you have administrator access.
2.  Reach out to your **RewardRally Account Manager** or our support team.
3.  Our team will coordinate with you and GoKwik to enable the synchronization and verify the connection.

Once our team completes the initial link, your GoKwik checkout will automatically begin recognizing and applying RewardRally store credits for your customers.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does this work with COD (Cash on Delivery)?**  
Yes, GoKwik specializes in COD optimization, and RewardRally credits can be applied to reduce the final amount due.

**How often does the balance sync?**  
The balance syncs in real-time. As soon as a transaction is completed or a refund is issued via GoKwik, the RewardRally wallet is updated.
