RewardRally offers multiple ways to synchronize your reward points with your Shopify checkout experience. Depending on your current setup, you can choose the integration that best fits your store.

## Available Ways to Sync

Select the method below that matches your store's setup to see the specific guide:

### 1. [Shopify Store Credit](/shopify/store-credit/native/)
The default option for stores using Shopify’s built-in store credit system. It provides a seamless, "real-money" experience at checkout.

### 2. [GoKwik Integration](/shopify/store-credit/gokwik/)
For stores using GoKwik’s one-click checkout and COD optimization. This ensures reward points are synced directly with the GoKwik checkout window.

---

### Which one should I choose?

*   If you primarily use **Shopify's default checkout**, we recommend the **Shopify Store Credit** integration.
*   If you use **GoKwik** to handle your checkout process and COD orders, choose the **GoKwik Integration**.

:::note
If you have **enabled Shopify Store Credit** as your source within the GoKwik dashboard, you must follow the [Shopify Store Credit setup guide](/shopify/store-credit/native/) to ensure your balances remain synchronized.
:::
